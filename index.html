About

I’m a graphic designer based in NYC who loves designing all kinds of creative work and is always excited to learn new things. I enjoy working on digital projects, visual identities, creative concepts, video editing for social media and content, and photography.

I like experimenting with typography, layout, color, motion, and visuals, and I’m always exploring new styles and ideas. I do not limit myself to just one type of design. I enjoy trying new formats, learning new tools, and pushing myself creatively with every project.

When I’m not designing, I love traveling, exploring new places, going to concerts and jazz shows, and finding inspiration from everyday life in the city.
